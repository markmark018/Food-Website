Types of Burgers

The Chicken Burger is a timeless favorite with a juicy chick patty, fresh lettuce, tomato, pickles, and a slice of cheese, all sandwiched between a soft, toasted bun.

The Cheeseburger adds an extra layer of indulgence with a slice of melted cheese atop the beef patty, creating a gooey, flavorful experience.

The Veggie Burger offers a delicious plant-based alternative for those who prefer vegetables or are looking for a healthier option.

Burger Recipes

Chicken Burger Recipe

  • Chicken
  • Salt and pepper
  • Burger buns
  • Cheese slices
  • Lettuce, tomato, pickles

Season the beef with salt and pepper, form into patties, and grill until cooked to your liking. Place on buns with cheese, lettuce, tomato, and pickles.

Veggie Burger Recipe

  • Veggie patty
  • Burger buns
  • Avocado
  • Tomato
  • Lettuce

Cook the veggie patties according to package instructions. Assemble on buns with avocado, tomato, and lettuce.

Cheese Burger Recipe

  • Ground beef
  • Burger buns
  • Salt and pepper
  • Tomato
  • Cheese

Season the ground beef with salt and pepper, then form into patties. Grill the patties until they reach your desired level of doneness. During the last minute of grilling, place a cheese slice on each patty to melt. Assemble the burgers on buns with lettuce, tomato, and pickles.
